## Why
The current local cache has only 300 one-minute candles per symbol. A quick offline check shows default MA/RSI/breakout settings are negative or no-trade after costs, and the MA optimizer accepts zero candidates on that cache.

## Scope
- Fetch and checksum at least 6-24 months of BTC-USDT and ETH-USDT data across 1m/5m/15m/1h bars.
- Label market regimes: trend, range, high/low volatility, spread/liquidity, event periods, bull/bear drawdowns.
- Run reproducible train/validation/test and rolling walk-forward reports per symbol/bar/strategy.
- Store a compact evidence matrix showing net PnL after costs, profit factor, drawdown, Sharpe/Sortino, trade count, baseline deltas, rejected orders, and accepted/rejected reason.
- Require no accepted config unless it passes in multiple regimes, not just one short sample.

## Ready when
We can point to a dated evidence artifact showing which strategy/bar/symbol combinations pass or fail and why.
