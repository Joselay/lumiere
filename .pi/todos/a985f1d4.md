## Why
A simple long-only MA crossover can churn in sideways markets and lose to fees/spread/slippage. To improve expected profitability, the bot needs strategy options that can avoid bad regimes and exploit different market behavior.

## Scope
- Add pluggable strategy interface so multiple strategies can share backtest, risk, and engine plumbing.
- Research/implement candidates such as trend-strength filter, volatility/ATR breakout, RSI mean-reversion, MACD confirmation, Bollinger-band reversion, and volume/liquidity filters.
- Add regime detection: trending vs ranging, high-volatility vs low-volatility, spread/liquidity acceptable vs unacceptable.
- Backtest each candidate with the same conservative cost model and walk-forward gates.

## Acceptance criteria
- At least 2 new strategy modules are covered by unit tests and backtest reports.
- Strategies can be selected by config without editing code.
- Reports show which market regimes each strategy is allowed to trade.
