## Why
The current `PaperTradingLedger` records simulated fills from decisions that are made against the real/demo account snapshot. If the live/demo account already holds BTC/ETH, paper can record sells without a paper cost basis, so the rolling performance gate can be misleading.

## Scope
- Add a true paper account/portfolio state per instrument: cash, positions, average cost, equity curve, fees, and realized/unrealized PnL.
- Feed strategies a paper `AccountSnapshot` when recording shadow decisions, not the live/demo account positions.
- Always collect paper evidence, even when `RISK_REQUIRE_PERFORMANCE_GATE=false`; enforcement should be a separate switch.
- Persist/reload paper state deterministically from JSONL.
- Add tests for restart, sells without inventory, multi-symbol state, and gate decisions.

## Ready when
A paper gate can only pass from a self-contained paper equity curve with valid cost basis, min trades, positive net PnL after costs, drawdown limits, and profit factor.

## Completion evidence
- Implemented event-sourced shadow portfolio in `src/lumiere/paper_trading.py` with cash, per-symbol positions/average cost, fees, realized/unrealized PnL, equity curve, deterministic replay, and rejection of sells without paper inventory.
- `TradingEngine` now records paper decisions from a paper `AccountSnapshot`; `build_engine` always creates a paper ledger while risk enforcement remains controlled by `RISK_REQUIRE_PERFORMANCE_GATE`.
- Added tests for restart/reload, no-inventory sell rejection, multi-symbol state, gate decisions, and engine paper/live account separation.
- Verification: `uv run ruff check .` and `uv run pytest -q`.
