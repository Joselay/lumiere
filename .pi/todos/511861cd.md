## Why
The project has structured logs and Telegram summaries, but there is no durable analytics store connecting signals -> risk decisions -> orders -> fills -> realized PnL. Without attribution we cannot tell whether losses come from bad signals, costs, slippage, sizing, or market regime.

## Scope
- Persist every candle snapshot, strategy input, decision, risk decision, order, fill, fee, spread, and account equity sample.
- Add daily/weekly reports for realized/unrealized PnL, fees, slippage, win rate, profit factor, drawdown, and baseline comparison.
- Add alerting for strategy decay: negative rolling expectancy, abnormal slippage, spread spikes, losing streak, or gate failure.
- Expose concise Telegram commands for recent trades and attribution.

## Acceptance criteria
- Restarting the bot does not lose PnL history.
- `/performance` can explain why money was won/lost over the selected window.
- Tests cover ledger persistence and report generation.
