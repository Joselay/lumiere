## Completed
- Added `lumiere-optimize` CLI for MA fast/slow grids, repeated bar sizes, per-asset trade sizes, cooldown metadata, and cost-model assumptions.
- Candidate reports are sorted by out-of-sample acceptance, net PnL, drawdown, profit factor, Sharpe/Sortino, trade count, and win rate.
- Promotion gates now require out-of-sample performance gates, positive OOS PnL, no-trade and buy-and-hold baseline outperformance, train/test divergence limits, optional walk-forward consistency, and optional parameter-stability neighbors.
- Optimizer writes reproducible `optimizer_report.json` and `accepted_candidates.json` artifacts.
- README documents the optimizer command and promotion gates.
