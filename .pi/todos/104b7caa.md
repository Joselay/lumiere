## Why
The only live strategy is fixed moving-average crossover (`fast=5`, `slow=20`). `strategy_evaluation.py` has early grid-evaluation primitives but no operator CLI or promotion workflow. A quick grid on the current 300x1m sample found only tiny BTC gains with 4 trades and ETH still negative, which is not enough evidence.

## Scope
- Add an optimizer CLI for MA windows, bar sizes, trade size, cooldown, and cost-model assumptions.
- Rank candidates by out-of-sample net PnL after costs, max drawdown, profit factor, Sharpe/Sortino, trade count, and win/loss distribution.
- Require candidates to beat no-trade and buy-and-hold baselines, not just be positive.
- Add anti-overfit rules: minimum trades, train/test divergence cap, walk-forward consistency, and parameter-stability checks.
- Save accepted candidate configs and reports as reproducible artifacts.

## Acceptance criteria
- `uv run ...` command produces a sorted candidate report and marks each candidate accepted/rejected with reason.
- No candidate can be promoted unless all out-of-sample gates pass.
- Tests cover accepted/rejected sorting and overfit rejection.
