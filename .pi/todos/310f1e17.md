## Why
The bot places market orders only. Even a profitable signal can become unprofitable after spread, slippage, fees, partial fills, and thin order book depth.

## Scope
- Add a pre-trade expected-edge check: do not trade unless expected move exceeds fees + spread + slippage + safety buffer.
- Use order book depth, not just top-of-book spread, to estimate executable size and slippage.
- Consider marketable-limit orders with timeout/cancel fallback instead of unconditional market orders.
- Track actual fill price vs decision candle price to measure realized slippage.
- Persist order/fill details needed for post-trade attribution.

## Acceptance criteria
- Risk blocks trades when estimated edge is less than estimated total cost.
- Backtests and paper mode model the same execution assumptions used by live risk.
- Telegram `/performance` or logs show spread, estimated cost, actual slippage, and rejected-by-cost counts.
